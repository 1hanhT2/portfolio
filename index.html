About Me

Hi, I’m Thanh — an English‑track student who loves building useful, human‑friendly tools. My background in competitive English sharpened how I think and communicate; my curiosity for technology turned that into products people can use.

I care about clarity, accessibility, and security. Whether it’s a listening‑test generator, a study timer, or a kanban board, I aim for simple interfaces, clean code, and sensible defaults.

I’m exploring the intersection of language and software — from technical writing and web apps to areas like NLP and educational technology.
