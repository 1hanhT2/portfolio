About Me

My name is Thanh, and I am an English Major at Quang Trung High School for the Gifted. My academic journey is driven by a dual passion: a deep fascination with the English language and a growing enthusiasm for web technology.

My interest in language extends to linguistics and etymology, where I enjoy analyzing the structure and history of words. This analytical mindset has helped me succeed in regional and national English competitions. At the same time, I am actively honing my front-end development skills (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and learning the fundamentals of web security, as I believe in building digital products that are both functional and secure.

I believe strong communication and technical literacy are essential in today's world. I am excited to find opportunities where I can apply both my command of English and my technical skills—whether in technical writing, accessible web development, or emerging fields like Natural Language Processing.
